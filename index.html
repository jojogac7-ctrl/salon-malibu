Our Story
Thirty Years of Refinement.

Established in 1995, Salon Malibu has been Nairobi's most distinguished hair and beauty destination for three decades. Located at Village Market in Gigiri — home to the United Nations and the diplomatic community — we have earned a reputation built on exceptional quality and unwavering standards.

Our international team of stylists combines decades of expertise with the world's finest product partners — Kérastase, Redken, Wella, René Guinot and Olaplex — to deliver results that are consistently, quietly outstanding.

We are appointment-only. Every visit is given the time, care and undivided attention it deserves.
